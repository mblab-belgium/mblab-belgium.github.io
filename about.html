Mathias Boeckx


My name is Mathias Boeckx, the "MB" behind the lab.

I’d like to share a bit more about myself:

I graduated as a construction equipment operator, which mainly involves operating and maintaining excavators and wheel loaders.

After secondary school, I worked for three years as a mechanic in a truck garage.

Looking for more challenge in my career, I decided to quit and return to school.

I’m currently studying for a Graduate Certificate in Internet of Things (IoT) at Thomas More in Geel.

The program appealed to me because of its broad scope and focus on topics such as networking, cloud computing, and embedded programming.

My hobbies include rock climbing (both indoors and outdoors), traveling, and working with electronics, including 3D printers.
